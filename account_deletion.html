Ultimate Cricket Coach – Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from Ultimate Cricket Coach, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

Email us at: your@email.com

Include your username in the email so we can identify your account.

What data is deleted

  • Your team and squad data
  • Your game progress
  • Your stored statistics

Data retention

All requested account data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of receiving your request.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at the email above.
